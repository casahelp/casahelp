Our Goal

Very often people rely on Google to find these small service providers. In order to get in touch with service provider it can be made around ten calls to ten different vendors. These actions are time consuming and very often are not effective both for customer and vendor.

The goal of Casa Help is to implement effective solution by helping customers discover, try and book trusted and reliable local services and professionals on the online platform/mobile application. Moreover, it will make it possible to book any required service at home starting from massage at home to a dietician at home.

Simply, it will be effectively organised home services marketplace.
